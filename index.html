Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with us

How long does it take to build an MVP? +

Typically, an MVP takes 3-8 weeks depending on complexity. We use React low-code solutions to accelerate development without compromising quality. During our free consultation, we'll provide a detailed timeline based on your specific requirements.

Do you provide design services? +

Yes! We work with experienced partner designers who create beautiful, user-friendly interfaces. We handle the entire process from wireframes to high-fidelity designs, ensuring your app not only works great but looks amazing too.

What's included in your development service? +

We provide full-cycle development: UI/UX design, web frontend development using React/Nextj.js, backend setup (Convex), payment integration (Stripe, RevenueCat), and post-launch support. Everything you need to go from idea to market.

Can you help with an existing app? +

Absolutely! We can take over existing projects, add new features, fix bugs, optimize performance, or completely rebuild your app using modern technologies. We'll start with a code audit to understand the current state and recommend the best path forward.

What are your pricing models? +

We offer flexible pricing: fixed-price for well-defined projects, time & materials for evolving requirements, or dedicated team for ongoing development. After understanding your needs during the free consultation, we'll recommend the best model and provide a detailed quote.

Do you offer post-launch support? +

Yes! We provide ongoing maintenance, bug fixes, performance monitoring, and feature updates. We're invested in your app's long-term success and will be there to help as your user base grows and your needs evolve.
